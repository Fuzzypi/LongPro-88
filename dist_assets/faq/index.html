Bed Bug Questions

How much does bed bug treatment cost in Cleveland? +

Bed bug treatment costs in the Cleveland area vary based on home size and infestation severity. We provide free, no-obligation estimates after a consultation. Our pricing is transparent — you'll know the full cost before any work begins. Contact us at (216) 300-4121 for a personalized quote.

How do I know if I have bed bugs? +

Common signs include waking up with itchy bites in clusters or lines, finding small reddish-brown spots on sheets, seeing tiny white eggs in mattress seams, or noticing a sweet musty odor. If you suspect bed bugs, contact us for a free inspection — early detection makes treatment faster and more effective.

How long does bed bug treatment take? +

Most bed bug treatments take 2-4 hours depending on home size. The majority of infestations are resolved in 1-2 treatments, with a follow-up inspection approximately 2 weeks after the initial treatment to verify complete elimination.

Will my neighbors know about my bed bug treatment? +

Absolutely not. We arrive in unmarked vehicles, wear plain clothing, and carry equipment in discreet containers. We never leave yard signs, door hangers, or any visible evidence of our visit. Your privacy is our top priority.

Do I need to throw away my mattress? +

In most cases, no. Professional treatment can effectively eliminate bed bugs from mattresses and furniture. We'll assess your specific situation during inspection and only recommend disposal if absolutely necessary.

Can bed bugs come back after treatment? +

Our treatment eliminates existing infestations. Re-infestation is possible if new bed bugs are introduced through travel, used furniture, or visitors. We provide prevention tips after every treatment and offer follow-up services if needed.

Preparation

How do I prepare my home for pest treatment? +

Preparation varies by pest type. For bed bugs, key steps include washing bedding in hot water, vacuuming thoroughly, clearing clutter from around beds, and ensuring access to all rooms. For general pest treatment, clear items from baseboards and remove pets. We provide a detailed checklist specific to your treatment when you schedule.

Do I need to leave my home during treatment? +

Yes, you and your pets should leave during treatment for the time specified by your technician — typically 2-4 hours. We'll provide exact timing based on the treatment method used.

Are your treatments safe for children and pets? +

We use products applied according to strict safety protocols. Children and pets should be removed during treatment and for the specified re-entry period. We provide specific safety guidance based on your household needs.
