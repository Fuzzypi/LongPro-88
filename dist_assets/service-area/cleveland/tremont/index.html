Historic Homes Attract Pests

Victorian-era homes are beautiful but have more entry points for pests. Older construction, foundation cracks, gaps around pipes, and compromised sealing create pathways for bed bugs, cockroaches, ants, and other invaders. We know exactly where to look and how to seal these vulnerabilities.

Apartment Buildings & Shared Walls

Tremont's mix of apartment buildings and converted townhouses means pests can spread between units quickly. Bed bugs in particular travel through walls and shared spaces. Our comprehensive treatments stop infestations at the source.

Restaurant District Proximity

Tremont's thriving restaurant and brewery scene is fantastic for residents but can attract cockroaches and other pests seeking food sources. Homes near dining establishments may face higher pest pressure, especially for German cockroaches.
