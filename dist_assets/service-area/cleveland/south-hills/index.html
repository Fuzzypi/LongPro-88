South Hills: A Neighborhood We Know

Historic Homes, Unique Challenges

South Hills is renowned for its beautiful Tudor-style homes, Cape Cods, and classic ranch properties. These architectural gems are home to 4,424 residents who value community and history. However, older homes can have hidden entry points, foundation cracks, and moisture issues that attract pests.

A Family-Friendly Community

South Hills is a quiet, family-friendly neighborhood with strong schools, community gardens, and excellent parks like John Muir Park. The neighborhood has rich Slovak and Ukrainian heritage and is known for its tight-knit, diverse community. You want your family safe from pests—we deliver that.

Location & Accessibility

Located near I-480 and the Jennings Freeway, just 6 miles south of downtown, South Hills is conveniently close to great schools, Memphis Kiddie Park, MetroHealth, and the Cleveland Metroparks Zoo. LongPro is based right next door in Old Brooklyn—your neighborhood pest control expert.
