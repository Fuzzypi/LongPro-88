Why Lakewood Has Unique Pest Challenges

Lakewood is the most densely populated city in Ohio, with over 50,000 residents packed into just 5.6 square miles. The city's housing stock is dominated by duplexes, doubles, and multi-family apartment buildings — many built in the early 1900s through the 1950s.

This density creates the perfect conditions for pest problems. Bed bugs, cockroaches, and other pests can move between connected units through shared walls, plumbing chases, and electrical conduits. A problem in one unit can quickly become a building-wide issue if not addressed professionally.

LongPro Pest Control has extensive experience treating Lakewood's unique housing types. We understand the construction methods used in these older buildings and know exactly where pests hide. Our BBB A+ accreditation since 2015 reflects our commitment to thorough, effective service.
