Our Story

LongPro was built for homeowners who wanted pest control to feel calm, private, and straightforward. When pests show up, people need clear answers and competent help, not pressure or theatrics.

From the start, we focused on residential service in Cleveland and nearby communities, with discreet arrivals, respectful communication, and realistic recommendations grounded in the condition of the home.

That mindset led to an inspection-first process. Before we recommend treatment, we look closely at where activity is happening, how pests are getting in, and what conditions are keeping the problem active.

Today, LongPro serves Greater Cleveland from the Old Brooklyn area. Our BBB A+ accreditation since 2015, 5.0 Google rating, and 17 public Google reviews reflect the same standard we've kept from day one: do the work carefully, explain it clearly, and protect the customer's privacy.

Our Inspection-First Approach

Every home presents a different pest problem, even when the pest is the same. A bed bug issue in a townhouse, a roach problem near a kitchen wall, and flea activity after a pet infestation all require different decisions.

That's why we start by inspecting first. We look for where pests are hiding, how they got into the home, what conditions are sustaining the activity, and what level of treatment makes sense.

We don't lead with a canned plan. We lead with evidence from the home, then explain our findings in plain language so you know exactly what we recommend and why.

The result is better targeting, fewer surprises, and a treatment plan designed for the real issue instead of the most obvious symptom.

The Discreet Difference

Discretion isn't just a feature of our service — it's the backbone of who we are. This is especially important when it comes to bed bugs, which carry a significant social stigma despite the fact that infestations have nothing to do with cleanliness or personal habits.

Bed bugs don't discriminate. They can happen to anyone, anywhere.

A single bed bug can travel on luggage, clothing, or furniture and start an infestation. It's not a reflection of your home, your hygiene, or your character. Yet many people suffer in silence rather than seek help, afraid of what neighbors or friends might think.

That's where LongPro's commitment to discretion comes in. Here's exactly how we protect your privacy:

  • ✓ Unmarked Vehicles — Our service vehicles carry no company name, logo, or identifying marks. We arrive quietly and leave quietly.
  • ✓ Plain Attire — Our technicians wear casual, unmarked clothing. No uniforms, no branded gear, no badges.
  • ✓ Discreet Equipment — All tools and equipment are stored in plain cases and bags that don't advertise pest control services.
  • ✓ No Signage — We never leave service notices, tent cards, or any visible signs of our visit on your property.
  • ✓ Confidential Communication — All invoices, receipts, and correspondence are discreet. We discuss your situation only with you, never with neighbors, landlords, or anyone else.

"Your pest problem is between you and us." That's our promise.
