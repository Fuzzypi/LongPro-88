About LongPro Pest Control

For over 13 years, LongPro Pest Control has been serving Greater Cleveland with discreet, effective pest control solutions. We understand that dealing with a pest problem can feel isolating and embarrassing. That's why we've built our entire business around one core principle: your pest problem is between you and us.

Our Story

LongPro Pest Control was born from a simple observation: people dealing with pest infestations often felt shame about asking for help. Whether it was bed bugs, cockroaches, or another pest issue, the stigma kept many Cleveland residents from reaching out until their problem became severe.

Over 13 years ago, we decided to change that. We founded LongPro Pest Control on the belief that pest control should be effective AND discreet. Everyone deserves to solve their pest problem without fear of judgment or embarrassment. We committed to building a company where discretion isn't an afterthought — it's the foundation of everything we do.

What started as a commitment to discretion evolved into something more: a detective's approach to pest control. We realized that the best results come from understanding the root cause, not just treating the symptoms. That's when "The Pest Detective" philosophy took shape, and it's guided every job we've done since.

Today, with 13+ years of experience, a BBB A+ rating, and hundreds of five-star customer reviews, we're proud to be Cleveland's most trusted name in discreet residential pest control. We've helped countless families reclaim their homes and their peace of mind — quietly, professionally, and completely confidentially.

The Pest Detective Approach

At LongPro Pest Control, pest control is investigation work. Our magnifying glass logo isn't just a symbol — it represents our methodical, detective-like approach to every job.

We don't believe in the "spray and pray" method. When you call us, we don't show up with a treatment plan already decided. Instead, we start with a thorough inspection. We look for where pests are hiding, how they got into your home, what conditions are attracting them, and what caused the problem in the first place. Only by understanding the complete picture can we develop a targeted strategy that actually works.

This detective work matters because every pest infestation is unique. A bed bug problem in a townhouse requires a different approach than one in a single-family home. A cockroach issue near your kitchen is fundamentally different from activity in a basement. By treating each case as its own investigation, we solve the right problem — not just the obvious one.

The result? Faster elimination, longer-lasting results, and fewer repeat calls. That's the power of investigating before treating.

The Discreet Difference

Discretion isn't just a feature of our service — it's the backbone of who we are. This is especially important when it comes to bed bugs, which carry a significant social stigma despite the fact that infestations have nothing to do with cleanliness or personal habits.

Bed bugs don't discriminate. They can happen to anyone, anywhere.

A single bed bug can travel on luggage, clothing, or furniture and start an infestation. It's not a reflection of your home, your hygiene, or your character. Yet many people suffer in silence rather than seek help, afraid of what neighbors or friends might think.

That's where LongPro's commitment to discretion comes in. Here's exactly how we protect your privacy:

  • ✓ Unmarked Vehicles — Our service vehicles carry no company name, logo, or identifying marks. We arrive quietly and leave quietly.
  • ✓ Plain Attire — Our technicians wear casual, unmarked clothing. No uniforms, no branded gear, no badges.
  • ✓ Discreet Equipment — All tools and equipment are stored in plain cases and bags that don't advertise pest control services.
  • ✓ No Signage — We never leave service notices, tent cards, or any visible signs of our visit on your property.
  • ✓ Confidential Communication — All invoices, receipts, and correspondence are discreet. We discuss your situation only with you, never with neighbors, landlords, or anyone else.

"Your pest problem is between you and us." That's our promise.
