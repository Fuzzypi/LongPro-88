Review Strategy Cheat Sheet

Goal: Get to 50+ reviews as fast as possible.

1. Ask EVERY customer. Most won't, but the ones who do add up fast. Even getting 2-3 per week gets you to 50+ in a few months.

2. Text immediately after service. Within 1-2 hours while they're grateful.

3. Make it personal. Use their name. Mention what you treated. "Hi Mike, glad we got those ants handled!"

4. Follow up once. 3 days later if no review. Just once — don't nag.

5. Respond to every review. Go to your GBP and reply to all 17 existing reviews. Google rewards active engagement.

6. Never offer incentives. Google bans reviews earned with discounts or freebies. Just ask honestly.
