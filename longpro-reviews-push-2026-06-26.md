## Rules that keep you safe (Google policy)
- **Never incentivize** reviews (no discounts, no entries-to-win, no freebies for a review). This can get reviews removed or the profile penalized.
- **Don't review-gate** — don't filter so only happy customers reach the review form (e.g., "press 1 if satisfied" funnels). Ask every customer; timing the ask around a good outcome is fine, screening who can review is not.
- **No bulk-blast** of dozens at once and no fake/family reviews — unnatural spikes get filtered.
- Ask from the customer's own device/account; don't leave reviews on shared shop devices.
