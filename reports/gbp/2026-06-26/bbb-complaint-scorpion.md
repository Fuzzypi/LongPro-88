## Complaint type
Service / Billing or contract issue — failure to release client property (Google Business Profile primary ownership) after termination of the marketing relationship.

## What the complaint is about (paste into the BBB "Describe your complaint" field)

Scorpion Home Services performed marketing services for my business, LongPro Pest Control LLC. During that engagement, Scorpion's account (`scorpionhomeservices2020@gmail.com`) was made the **Primary Owner** of my company's Google Business Profile — the free Google listing that controls how my business appears in Google Search and Google Maps.

The marketing relationship has ended. My company-controlled Google account (`longpropc@gmail.com`) remains only an "Owner" on the profile, while Scorpion's account retains Primary Owner control. Primary Owner is the highest level of control: it can remove all other owners and managers, including mine.

I have requested in writing, three separate times, that Scorpion transfer Primary Ownership to my company account:

- **May 8, 2026** — initial written request to transfer primary ownership to `longpropc@gmail.com`.
- **May 22, 2026** — final notice giving 5 business days to act.
- **June 22, 2026** — third notice; advised I would file a Google ownership dispute and a BBB complaint.

Each request included the exact, free, two-minute steps to make the transfer (People and access → set `longpropc@gmail.com` to Primary owner). **Scorpion has not responded to any of the three requests and has not made the transfer.**

This is my business's own listing, tied to my licensed Ohio business, my phone number, and reviews left by my customers. Scorpion's continued retention of Primary Ownership after the relationship ended, and its refusal to respond to repeated written transfer requests, is leaving control of my business's most important online listing in the hands of a former vendor against my wishes.

## Resolution sought (paste into "Desired resolution" / "Desired settlement")

Transfer **Primary Ownership** of the LongPro Pest Control LLC Google Business Profile from `scorpionhomeservices2020@gmail.com` to `longpropc@gmail.com`, and confirm in writing that no owners or managers controlled by LongPro will be removed. Desired settlement type: **Other (correct a business practice).**

## Exact transfer steps (include so Scorpion cannot claim it's burdensome)

1. Sign in to Google Business Profile with `scorpionhomeservices2020@gmail.com`.
2. Open the profile for **LongPro Pest Control LLC**.
3. Go to **Business Profile settings → People and access**.
4. Click `longpropc@gmail.com`.
5. Change the role to **Primary owner**.
